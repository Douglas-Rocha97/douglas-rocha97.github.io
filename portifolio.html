Projects

Foodly

mobile app that helps users find recipe ideas based on the ingredients they already have at home. By scanning an image of your fridge or pantry, Foodly identifies available ingredients and suggests recipes tailored to your cooking time — from quick 15-minute meals to more elaborate dishes that take 45+ minutes.

Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript(Stimulus.js)

Back-end: Ruby on Rails

MailAi

A chatbot that allows users to choose their desired level of politeness and formats emails accordingly with AI assistance. Especially useful in Japanese work environments, where the vocabulary used with colleagues differs significantly from the language used with leaders and customers.

Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript(Stimulus.js)

Back-end: Ruby on Rails
